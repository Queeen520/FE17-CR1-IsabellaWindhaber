Simple Apple Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 apples
  • 150g butter or margarine
  • 150g of sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pack vanilla flavor custard powder
  • 250g flour
  • 3 teaspoons, leveled baking powder
  • 5 tbsp milk
  • fat and flour for the mold
  • powdered sugar for dusting

Making Up

  1. Peel and quarter the apples, cut out the core and dice the flesh.
  2. Beat the fat, sugar and salt with the hand mixer until white and creamy.
    Beat in eggs one at a time.
  3. Mix pudding powder, flour and baking powder. Fold the flour mixture and milk
    alternately into the batter. Fold the apples into the batter.
    Pour the batter into a greased, floured springform pan (26cm) and smooth out.
    Bake the cake in the preheated oven (electric stove: 175 °C /
    circulating air: 150 °C) for approx. 45 minutes.
  4. Remove the cake from the oven, let cool on a wire rack and remove from the tin.
    Dust with powdered sugar.
