What's the secret behinde Food Blog ?


We startet some years ago with this Blog just to have a place where we can store
our most loved recipes and also share it with our Friends and Family.
Than we added our most favorite restaurants in the city and stuff like that.
It didn't took a long time since people startet to ask about the places and recipes and also wantet to share their content.


And so we startet to Co-Work with Food Blogger or just Food Lover from all around the world!
We don't do it for money and we also don't pay our Bloggers.
Everyone can write us and share content if it fits to our website!


We proudly can say that we don't earn money that we keep!

We never wantet to earn money with this blog, so it's importat to us
that you know, everything we earn goes to the Feed The World Foundation!

But we think that we all could do more to stop hunger around the world!
So we decided to something new this year. We will do a FoundRaising next Month!
(More Informations will follow soon)
We just ask everyone to give as much as is possible for you in your situation!
Every donation counts!
